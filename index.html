Handi Haat

A mobile app platform to allow villagers to sell their products across the globe

How Handi Haat is different than a regular E-commerce app?

Ans -Well Handi Haat uses ML, Computer Vision, AR to full context apart from a basic Ecommerce

How we are using Computer Vision and AR?

Ans -well with help of Computer Vision we are able to provide a virtual version reality where users are able to feel how handicrafts will be shown in their home or how a certain dress will look on

Use of ML(Machine Learning)?

Ans -We are using Machine Learning for the Item-Item recommendation and also using unspervised learning to detect the warehouse and the main hub of the market
